Creamy Garlic Pasta

Perfect for a quick lunch or an easy dinner.

A smooth and comforting pasta loaded with rich garlic flavor. Simple ingredients come together for a restaurant-style dish at home.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Cream (or milk)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Grated cheese (parmesan)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente; reserve some pasta water.
  2. Heat butter and olive oil, saut√© garlic until fragrant (do not brown).
  3. Add cream, salt, pepper, and chili flakes; simmer gently.
  4. Toss in pasta, add pasta water if needed, mix well.
  5. Finish with grated cheese and serve warm.
